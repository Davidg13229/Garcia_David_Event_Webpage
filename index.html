Green Future Summit 2025: Rethinking Waste, Restoring Earth

A premier event focused on transforming waste into resources and shaping a sustainable future. Through expert discussions and innovative solutions, the summit will empower businesses and individuals to minimize waste and adopt eco-friendly practices.

Date & Location:

August 17, 2025 2:30 GMT-7

Zoom Meeting

About The Event:

The GreenFuture Summit 2025 is a global conference dedicated to tackling waste management challenges and advancing sustainable solutions. It aims to bring together experts, policymakers, and eco-entrepreneurs to drive innovation in circular economy practices, sustainable packaging, and waste reduction strategies.


Key Objectives:

  • Promote zero-waste solutions for businesses and individuals
  • Explore eco-friendly innovations in waste management
  • Educate on the impact of plastic pollution & e-waste
  • Foster collaboration between governments, corporations, and NGOs

Meet the Speakers

Dr. Maria Chen

Environmental Scientist, UN Climate Task Force (2018 - Present)

Dr. Chen brings extensive experience in developing eco-friendly policies that align with global sustainability goals. She will highlight how waste-to-resource technologies can be leveraged for climate resilience and discuss the role of policy-driven innovation in achieving a sustainable future.

Field of Knowledge:

  • Climate Science & Environmental Sustainability
  • Policy-making in Climate Change
  • Sustainable Waste Solutions

James Carter

The CEO of EcoTech Innovationst (2019 - Present)

With a background in eco-tech entrepreneurship, Carter will showcase the latest green technologies that are revolutionizing recycling and sustainable production. He will also discuss how biodegradable materials and smart waste management systems are shaping the future of sustainability.

Field of Knowledge:

  • Green Technology & AI-driven Waste Management
  • Circular Economy & Business Sustainability
  • Eco-friendly Startups & Innovation

Amina Patel

Founder of ZeroWaste Global (2015 - Present)

As a sustainability advocate, Patel will discuss the long-term impact of plastic pollution, effective methods for reducing single-use plastics, and how businesses can transition to carbon-neutral operations. Her insights will be backed by real-world success stories and industry best practices.

Field of Knowledge:

  • Zero-Waste Strategies for Households & Businesses
  • Sustainable Consumer Behavior & Waste Reduction
  • Global Waste Impact & Environmental Advocacy

Venue

Where will the venue be held?

The event will be held virtually via Zoom, allowing participants from around the world to join seamlessly.

Registered participants will receive a secure Zoom link via email before the event.

Why Virtual?

The Sustainable Future Summit goes virtual to reduce carbon emissions, minimize waste, and lower energy consumption.

This eco-friendly approach enhances global accessibility while showcasing how technology drives sustainable solutions for a greener future.
